FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Register" tab above and filling out the form with your details.

Follow these steps to create your account:

  • Choose a valid and accessible email address.
  • Create a strong password (8+ characters, mixed letters, numbers, and symbols).
  • Provide your first name, last name, and optionally a username.
  • Submit the form, then click the confirmation link received in your email.

Once confirmed, your account will be immediately active with a personalized dashboard, exclusive offers, activity tracking, and real-time notifications. You can also link your account to your social networks for easier access.

How do I edit my profile?

Once logged in, you can access and edit your profile by clicking on your username in the menu.

To customize your profile:

  • Change your photo (JPG/PNG, max 2 MB).
  • Add a biography (up to 500 characters).
  • Set your interests and notification preferences.

Once saved, a confirmation message will appear. If an error occurs, a notification will guide you to correct the missing or improperly formatted fields.

How do I participate in tournaments with my account?

Creating an account allows you to easily register for tournaments and track your progress.

Registration procedure:

  • Go to the "Events" section and select the tournament you are interested in.
  • Click "Register" and fill in the required details (team, level, availability).
  • Submit and receive a confirmation email with your participant number.

Match tracking:

  • Check your dashboard to view opponents and schedules.
  • Receive real-time results after each match.
  • Access detailed statistics and replays if available.

Where to Find Us

Where is Chroma Café located?

We are at 14 Rue d'Enghien, 33000 Bordeaux, just a stone's throw from Gambetta Square.

Access and surroundings:

  • Historic center, near Gambetta Square.
  • Pedestrian streets and a shaded terrace.
  • Bike charging stations and public benches nearby.

The interior is cozy, with digital artworks on the walls and a welcoming workspace.

What are your opening hours?

We are open from Tuesday to Saturday, 9 AM to 7 PM; Sunday from 10 AM to 4 PM.

Special hours:

  • Brunch on Saturdays & Sundays, from 11 AM to 2 PM.
  • Artistic gatherings on Tuesday evenings (6 PM–9 PM).
  • Late-night sessions for private events.

Updates (holidays, special events) are posted on our website and social media.

How do I get here by public transport?

The nearest tram station is "Hôtel de Ville" (line A), a 3-minute walk away.

Available options:

  • Tram A to "Hôtel de Ville", then 150 m on foot.
  • Buses 4, 5, 9 to Gambetta Square (200 m away).
  • Bike-sharing and taxis available at Saint-Jean station exit.

For travel from the station, take Tram C to "Quinconces", then Tram A to "Hôtel de Ville".

Is there parking nearby?

Yes, the "Victor Hugo" public parking is 200 m away on Vital Carles Street.

Services and rates:

  • Covered/uncovered spots with decreasing hourly rates.
  • Monthly subscriptions for regular users.
  • EV charging stations available 24/7.

"Galeries" parking (400 m away) offers daily packages, and free blue zones are available before 9 AM each morning.

Tournament Questions

How do I join a tournament?

Go to the "Events" section, select a tournament, and click "Register." You must be logged in to confirm.

Registration steps:

  • Check eligibility criteria (age, ranking).
  • Fill in your details (team, level, availability).
  • Receive a reminder 24 hours before the start via SMS or email.

During the tournament, use the integrated chat to organize your matches. An online referee validates each result and publishes detailed statistics.

How do I track my results?

Your matches and scores are available in your profile under the "My Tournaments" tab.

Real-time tracking:

  • Pop-up notifications after each match.
  • A complete history (wins, losses, average duration).
  • CSV export for analysis and sharing on social media.

Digital badges reward your achievements (10 wins, 50 matches, etc.) and are displayed on your profile.

Can I organize my own tournament?

Yes, go to the organizer dashboard and click "Create a tournament" to set up the details.

Tournament creation:

  • Define the name, date, format (1v1, team) and entry fees.
  • Customize rules, categories (beginner, expert), and rewards.
  • Add a logo, description, and attractive visuals.

Monitor registrations in real time, manage disputes, and generate automatic reports to ensure a smooth competition.
