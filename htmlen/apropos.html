About Chroma

About Chroma

Welcome to Chroma, a new kind of cybercoffee where technology, entertainment, and culture converge. More than just an internet coffee, Chroma is a hybrid living space that brings together gaming enthusiasts, music lovers, and pop culture fans in a warm environment. Our goal is to build a united community where everyone—whether a competitive gamer, a K-pop fan, or simply a coffee aficionado—feels at home, shares their passions, and makes new connections.

Conceived as an innovative, welcoming space, Chroma combines three worlds in one. Whether you’re enjoying a latte with friends, competing in a thrilling video game tournament, or singing along to the latest K-pop hits at a themed party, this unique blend makes Chroma an exceptional and dynamic venue powered by the spirit of sharing, creativity, and community passion.

The coffee Section – Comfort & Relaxation

Chroma’s coffee is the perfect spot to relax and recharge. Enjoy a cappuccino, a fragrant tea, or a refreshing beverage prepared by our baristas in a cozy, colorful ambiance. Whether you’re taking a break between games or simply need free wifi to work or study, our coffee welcomes you from morning until night.

The E-sport Section – Competition & Adrenaline

Get ready for action in our E-sport area! Chroma provides you with a true gaming arena, equipped with ultra-performance PCs, next-gen consoles, and high-definition screens to experience your games fully. Whether you're a casual gamer or a dedicated competitor, you'll find everything you need to quench your passion for video games in a friendly setting.

  • High-end equipment: gaming PCs, consoles, and ergonomic chairs.

  • Tournaments & competitions: regular events on FPS, MOBA, sports, etc.

  • Coaching & training: coaching sessions by experienced players.

  • Broadcasts: live streams of major international e-sport competitions.

The K-pop Section – Passion & Community

Dive into the heart of K-pop culture in the most exciting space at Chroma! This universe is dedicated to fans of Asian music and pop culture, offering a rich lineup of events to celebrate your passion together. Enjoy concert live streams, themed parties, and a lounge area decorated in the colors of your favorite idols.

  • Themed parties: artist birthdays, dance challenges, music quizzes.

  • Concert broadcasts: live or recorded streams on giant screens.

  • Fan club area: a cozy corner to chat, share news, and photos.

  • Asian culture: decor, playlists, and Korean snacks for a complete immersion.

Combining a relaxed coffee, e-sport, and K-pop, Chroma is more than just a place: it's a unique experience in Bordeaux. Join us and live your passions in a friendly and exciting environment!
